# Sortd - AI scheduler/calendar all-in-one

# What is Sortd?
Sortd is an AI-powered weekly planner built specifically for students. Unlike generic productivity apps like Notion or Google Calendar — which are built for adults in offices — Sortd understands the actual chaos of being a student. It knows that you have footy training on Tuesday, you're cooked by Thursday, there's a maths exam on Friday, and somehow a birthday party on Saturday. It reasons around all of that, not just your homework. The AI speaks like a friend giving you real advice, not a corporate productivity tool telling you to "optimise your workflow." You add your tasks, exams, sport commitments, and social events — and Sortd builds you a realistic plan that actually fits your life. It can also search the web to find tutors, GP clinics, study resources, and more without you ever leaving the app. Everything is colour-coded by type, priority-aware, and built around how students actually think and work.

# How to use it
Start by adding your tasks in the left sidebar under the "Add task" tab — give each one a name, pick whether it's an assignment, exam, study session, sport, or social event, set a priority level, and choose a date and time. If you're in a hurry, use the quick-add preset buttons at the top to instantly fill in common tasks like an exam or training session. Once added, your tasks appear directly on the calendar as colour-coded blocks — red for exams, blue for assignments, purple for sport, amber for social, and green for study sessions. You can drag events between days to move them around, or click any empty slot on the calendar to pre-fill that time in the form. Clicking any event opens an options panel where you can mark it done, reschedule it, edit it, delete it, or ask the AI for advice about it. If you add two tasks at the same time, Sortd automatically detects the clash and moves the lower-priority one out of the way — if they're the same priority, it asks you which one should move. The "My tasks" tab in the sidebar shows all your tasks in a list with filters, stats, and a week load indicator. The "AI chat" tab lets you ask anything — find free time, reschedule something because plans changed, search for a GP clinic near you, find a year 10 maths tutor, or get study tips for a specific subject. Use the plan tier buttons at the top to preview what Pro, Plus, and School plans unlock, including web search, AI tutoring, exam countdown mode, group study scheduling, and more.
